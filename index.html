Frequently Asked Questions

How much does CarHaggling cost?

We charge a simple $299 upfront fee to deploy your AI co-pilot. This covers our comprehensive market analysis, vehicle vetting, and negotiation services. You'll typically save 3-5x this amount through our expert negotiation process.

What if I don't find a car I like?

While our $299 fee covers the comprehensive analysis and negotiation services, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our service within 30 days, we'll provide a full refund.

How does the AI negotiation work?

Our AI analyzes thousands of data points including market prices, seller behavior patterns, and vehicle history to determine the optimal negotiation strategy. It communicates with sellers on your behalf, presenting offers that maximize your savings while maintaining a professional relationship.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve major metropolitan areas across the United States, including California, Texas, New York, Florida, and Illinois. We're rapidly expanding to new markets based on demand.

How long does the process take?

From initial consultation to final purchase, the average process takes 2-4 weeks. However, some customers have found their perfect car in as little as 48 hours. The timeline depends on your specific requirements and market availability.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent, and all communications are conducted through secure channels.

What types of vehicles can you help me find?

We can help you find any type of vehicle - from compact cars to luxury vehicles, SUVs, trucks, electric vehicles, and more. Our AI is trained on all major makes and models, and we work with both new and used vehicles from dealerships and private sellers.

Do you handle financing and insurance?

While we focus on finding and negotiating the best vehicle price, we can connect you with our partner lenders and insurance providers for competitive rates. However, you're free to use your own financing and insurance if you prefer.

What if there are issues with the car after purchase?

We thoroughly vet all vehicles before presenting them to you, including VIN checks, history reports, and preliminary inspections. However, if issues arise after purchase, we'll help you navigate warranty claims and work with the seller to resolve any problems.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Yes! We can help you get the best value for your trade-in. Our AI analyzes market data to ensure you receive a fair trade-in value, and we'll negotiate this as part of your overall deal.

How do you ensure I'm getting the best price?

Our AI continuously monitors market prices, analyzes thousands of similar sales, and tracks pricing trends. We compare your deal against historical data and current market conditions to ensure you're getting the best possible price for your specific vehicle and location.

What makes CarHaggling different from other car buying services?

Unlike traditional car buying services, we use advanced AI that learns from every negotiation to continuously improve. We don't just find cars - we analyze market data, predict pricing trends, and negotiate like an expert. Our transparent $299 upfront fee covers all services, and our satisfaction guarantee ensures you're completely happy with the results.
